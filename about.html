How I think about growth

A few things I believe
that most agencies don't.

🔍
Look before you optimise
Most agencies run audits after they've already decided what to sell you. I look at what's actually happening first — in the data, in the user behaviour, in the competitive landscape — before recommending anything.
⚙️
Systems beat campaigns
A campaign gets results once. A system gets results every week, compounding over time. Everything I build is designed to keep running without constant manual effort — that's what AI automation enables.
📊
Revenue is the only metric that matters
Rankings, impressions, sessions — these are inputs, not outcomes. I report on what's actually in your bank account. If the work isn't moving revenue, we need to talk about why, not send you a prettier report.
