# Outbound Message Sequences
**Vanguardeer — Prospecting Templates**

*Usage notes:*
- *Personalise every message. Replace all [brackets] with specific details.*
- *Do not send all three messages in quick succession. Space them 3–5 business days apart.*
- *If the prospect responds at any stage, exit the sequence and continue the conversation manually.*
- *Always send from a personalised account — not a mass email tool.*

---

## Sequence 1: Private Clinics

**Target:** Clinic owners, medical directors, clinic managers at aesthetic, dental, fertility, specialist, or health screening clinics.

---

### Message 1 — Initial observation

**Channel:** Email or LinkedIn DM

**Subject (email):** Quick observation about [Clinic Name]

**Body:**
> Hi [Name],
>
> I was reviewing clinics in [aesthetic / dental / fertility / specialist] in [Singapore / location] and noticed a few places where competitors appear to be capturing more patient trust before appointments are even booked.
>
> This usually isn't just an SEO issue. It tends to show up across local visibility, review strength, treatment page clarity, and booking journey friction — all before the patient ever contacts the clinic.
>
> Worth sending over a short snapshot of what I noticed for [Clinic Name]?
>
> Azam
