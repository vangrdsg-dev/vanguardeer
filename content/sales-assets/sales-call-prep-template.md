## Questions to Ask

### Opening
1. "Before we start — what prompted you to fill in the brief at this particular time?"
2. "Tell me a bit more about the business. How long have you been operating and what's driving growth right now?"

### Diagnosing the problem
3. "Where do most leads or enquiries currently come from?"
4. "When you look at competitors who seem to be capturing more of the market — what do you think they're doing differently?"
5. "What have you tried before in terms of marketing or growth? What worked, what didn't?"
6. "What is one new client / patient / customer worth to you approximately — both upfront and over time?"
7. "What's your current close rate on enquiries that come in?"

### Understanding urgency
8. "Why is this a priority right now, rather than 6 months from now?"
9. "What does it cost you every month that this isn't solved?"

### Qualification
10. "Who else is involved in making a decision on this?"
11. "If the diagnostic shows a clear opportunity, what would need to be true for you to move forward?"

### Budget
12. "Have you budgeted for this kind of engagement, or is that a conversation we'd need to have?"

### Closing
13. "If the case is strong — and we believe it usually is for the kind of business you run — is there any reason you wouldn't move forward within the next 30 days?"

## Objections to Prepare For

### "Too expensive"
> "Compared to what specifically? One missed high-value client per month is usually worth more than the engagement. Let me show you how we think about the numbers."

### "We already have an SEO agency"
> "That may be true. But if visibility, trust, enquiry quality, and sales readiness are still weak, then you have SEO activity, not a revenue system. The question is whether the leakage is commercially significant enough to fix."

### "Can you guarantee results?"
> "No credible operator should guarantee rankings, leads, or revenue — those depend on your market, your offer, competition, and execution. What we guarantee is that we build the strongest controllable system and operate it with discipline. The results follow that."

### "We just need more leads"
> "Maybe. But if the buyer journey is weak, more leads may just create more admin. First we need to diagnose whether the constraint is visibility, trust, conversion, or sales readiness."

### "We need to think about it"
> "Understood. What specifically needs to be resolved — the problem, the approach, the investment, timing, or internal approval? I want to make sure I can give you what you need to make the decision."

### "We have an internal team"
> "That can be a real advantage. We don't replace execution that works. We provide the system, strategy, and AI-assisted workflows that make internal resources more commercially effective."

### "Not ready now"
> "That's fine. When do you expect the timing to be right? And is there anything I can send over in the meantime that would be useful when you are ready?"
