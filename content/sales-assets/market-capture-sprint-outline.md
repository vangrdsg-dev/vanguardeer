# Market Capture Sprint — Programme Outline
**Vanguardeer | 12-Week Revenue System Implementation**

---

## Overview

The Market Capture Sprint is a focused 12-week implementation programme designed for high-value expert businesses that already have market demand but are losing qualified opportunities because their buyer journey is weak, fragmented, or founder-dependent.

**Duration:** 12 weeks  
**Engagement type:** Fixed-scope implementation sprint  
**Starting price:** From S$15,000 (scope depends on market, complexity, and implementation requirements)  
**Kickoff:** Typically within 2–3 weeks of signing

---

## Who It Is For

- Private clinics, specialist practices, and healthcare businesses
- Cybersecurity, MSP, and B2B IT firms
- Premium advisory and consulting firms
- Corporate service and market entry businesses
- Any high-value expert business where one missed opportunity is commercially significant

---

## Programme Architecture

### Phase 1: Diagnose (Weeks 1–3)

**Objective:** Map exactly where demand is leaking and what needs to be fixed first.

**Deliverables:**
- Revenue leakage audit (full)
- Competitor capture map (3 main competitors)
- Buyer journey review (search → enquiry → sale)
- Search and local visibility review
- Website conversion friction review
- Trust signal gap analysis
- Lead handling and qualification review
- Sales readiness assessment

**Output:** Priority Revenue Leak Report with implementation roadmap

---

### Phase 2: Build (Weeks 4–7)

**Objective:** Build the foundational assets and systems that capture demand more effectively.

**Deliverables (selected based on Phase 1 priorities):**
- Positioning refinement and messaging architecture
- Landing page or core page improvements
- Trust asset recommendations and implementation
- Review and reputation workflow setup
- Lead capture structure and CTA architecture
- Qualification form design
- Tracking foundation (GA4 events, form tracking)
- Competitor intelligence baseline

**Output:** Improved buyer journey with measurable conversion architecture in place

---

### Phase 3: Automate (Weeks 8–10)

**Objective:** Install AI-assisted workflows that reduce founder dependency and improve execution cadence.

**Deliverables:**
- AI-assisted lead research workflow
- AI-assisted follow-up support templates
- CRM pipeline setup or refinement (or structured manual process if no CRM)
- Sales-call briefing template
- Competitor monitoring workflow
- Content support workflow

**Output:** Operating layer that supports qualification, follow-up, and sales readiness without manual effort per lead

---

### Phase 4: Optimise (Weeks 11–12)

**Objective:** Review performance, refine the system, and identify next-phase priorities.

**Deliverables:**
- Weekly execution review (final 2 weeks)
- Conversion friction analysis and quick improvements
- Offer clarity improvements based on lead quality data
- Lead quality analysis (what's working, what needs adjustment)
- Monthly market capture report (first edition)
- 90-day post-sprint roadmap

**Output:** Sprint close document with optimised system and clear next-phase roadmap

---

## What the Sprint Is Designed to Improve

The sprint is designed to help the business:

- Become easier to find in relevant search channels
- Become easier to trust through stronger proof and credibility signals
- Become easier to contact with a lower-friction enquiry journey
- Improve the quality of incoming enquiries
- Reduce cold sales conversations by pre-educating prospects
- Strengthen follow-up discipline and consistency
- Identify and monitor competitor market position
- Build a repeatable acquisition foundation that improves over time

*Note: The sprint does not guarantee specific rankings, revenue figures, lead volumes, or conversion rates. Results depend on market conditions, competition, offer strength, execution discipline, and factors outside Vanguardeer's direct control.*

## Client Responsibilities

For the sprint to be effective, the client is expected to:

- Assign a primary contact for weekly check-ins
- Provide access to relevant systems (website, Google Business Profile, CRM if applicable)
- Review and approve content and copy before publication
- Implement technical changes if outside Vanguardeer's direct access
- Respond to review and reputation management workflow (where applicable)
- Attend weekly or fortnightly progress calls (30–45 minutes)

## After the Sprint

Clients who complete the Market Capture Sprint typically transition to one of:

1. **AI Revenue Operating System** — ongoing monthly execution, optimisation, and intelligence
2. **Quarterly optimisation retainer** — lighter ongoing support with quarterly deep reviews
3. **Independent operation** — client operates the system internally using the sprint deliverables

The sprint is designed to produce a system that can be operated independently, but most clients find ongoing execution support valuable.
