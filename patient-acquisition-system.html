The Patient Decision Journey

Patients compare trust before they compare treatment.

Before a patient contacts a clinic, they have usually searched, compared, read reviews, checked credentials, evaluated convenience, and judged whether the clinic feels safe. If your competitors appear stronger at this stage, they win before your team speaks to the patient.

  • Competitors appear more often in local and organic search
  • Review volume or recency is weaker than competing clinics
  • Treatment pages do not answer patient concerns clearly
  • The booking or enquiry path creates unnecessary friction
  • WhatsApp or contact handling is inconsistent
  • Patient education is thin or too generic
  • The clinic looks clinically capable but commercially under-positioned
