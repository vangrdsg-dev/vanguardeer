The Revenue Leak Intelligence Brief shows where prospects are finding, trusting, contacting, or choosing your competitors instead of you — across visibility, trust signals, conversion, and sales readiness.

What This Is

This is not a generic SEO audit.

The Revenue Leak Intelligence Brief is a focused market-capture review for high-value expert businesses that want to understand where visibility, trust, conversion, or sales-readiness gaps may be costing them qualified opportunities.

It is selective. We review every application to determine whether there is a strong enough commercial case before preparing the brief. Not every applicant will receive one.

What the brief reviews

  • Market visibility — where you appear vs. where demand exists
  • Competitor positioning — who is capturing demand and how
  • Search and local presence — visibility in the channels buyers use
  • Website conversion friction — what is stopping visitors from enquiring
  • Trust signals — reviews, proof, authority, credibility gaps
  • Lead capture journey — how enquiries arrive and at what quality
  • Sales readiness — how prepared the buyer is before speaking to you
  • Priority opportunities — the 3–5 highest-impact fixes

This is not for

  • ✕ Businesses looking for cheap SEO or social media posting
  • ✕ Businesses not ready to invest in implementation if the case is strong
  • ✕ Businesses where no single client or patient has meaningful commercial value
  • ✕ Applications without a decision-maker involved
